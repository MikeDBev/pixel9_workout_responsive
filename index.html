Weekly Bodyweight Plan

Weekly Schedule

  • 3–4 days/week: Monday, Wednesday, Friday, Sunday
  • Active recovery (walk, gentle stretch) on off‑days
  • 3 sets per exercise · 30–60 s rest between sets
  • Warm‑up: 5 min brisk walk/jog in place
  • Cool‑down: 5 min stretching

Workout Routine

Jumping Jacks (1 min)

Great warm‑up for cardiovascular health and calorie burn.

Bodyweight Squats (15–20 reps)

Engages legs & core; boosts metabolism.

Push‑Ups (10–15 reps)

Strengthens upper body & core.

Plank (30–60 s)

Excellent for core strength and belly‑fat reduction.

Mountain Climbers (30 s)

Full‑body cardio emphasizing abs.

Reverse Lunges (12–15 each leg)

Strengthens legs, glutes & core stability.

Bicycle Crunches (15–20 each side)

Directly targets abdominal muscles.
